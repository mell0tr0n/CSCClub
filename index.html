Computer Science & Coding Club

We're here to provide SDCCD students who are interested in computer science and/or coding with:

  • learning and co-working opportunities via workshops and projects
  • resources for internships, employment, and transferring to a 4-year college or university
  • Insight from industry professionals
  • Support and community

Possible topics to be covered in workshops:

  • How to use Git / GitHub, a version tracking software useful to programmers of all types
  • Basics of using/programming Raspberry Pi microcontrollers and possible applications
  • Basics of HTML & CSS
  • Overview of popular Front End, Back End, and Full Stack frameworks
  • Coding Bootcamps and their alternatives
  • Open Source, what it is and how to get involved
  • Studying and Time Management Techniques
  • <Your Interest Here>

Possible programs for co-work:

  • Making videos/educational resources for tech use (using keyboard shortcuts, how to back up files, how to use a password manager, etc)
  • Club website
  • Individual portfolio websites
  • Task organizer app

Who Can Join?

ANYONE who is a current SDCCD student, from total beginners to pros. All are welcome!

When Do We Meet?

The club will meet virtually at least once a month, with additional, optional Covid-safe in-person meetings if there is interest. Schedule TBD.

How Do I Join?

Send us a message at csccmesa@gmail.com including your full name and SDCCD student ID number. Tell us you want in and we'll add you to the list and send you an invite to our Discord server.
